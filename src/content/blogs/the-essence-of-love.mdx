# The Essence of Love

Love is the thread that weaves through every meaningful moment of our lives.

## What Is Love?

It's not a single emotion — it's a practice. A choice made daily, in small moments and grand gestures alike.

## Finding It in the Ordinary

The morning coffee made just right. A message that arrives at exactly the right time. The quiet comfort of presence.

These are love's truest expressions.
